Jerk Chicken

Homemade Super Easy Jerk Chicken Recipe

This is one of the best marinade recipes you’ll ever try! It’s brimming with fresh flavor and it may seem like a strang
> combination of ingredients but together they create incredible, well balanced flavor. And it all compliments chicken
perfectly.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces- 5 thighs and 5 legs
  • Green onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Habanero peppers
  • Lime juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Thyme leaves
  • Black pepper
  • All spice, cinammon and nutmeg

Steps

  1. Place chicken pieces in a gallon size resealable bag.
  2. Make marinade: add green onions, garlic, ginger, habaneros, lime juice, soy sauce, brown sugar, thyme, and spices
    to a food processor and plus to chop (it should be slightly coarse not pureed).
  3. Pour marinade over chicken: pour mixture over chicken in bag, seal bag while pressing out excess air then rub
    marinade over chicken.
  4. Allow marinating time: rest in refrigerator 3 – 24 hours.
  5. Heat oven and prepare baking sheet: preheat oven to 375 degrees. Line a 18 by 13-inch baking sheet with aluminum
    foil and spray with non-stick cooking spray.
  6. Arrange marinated chicken on sheet: remove chicken from marinade and arrange pieces on baking sheet leaving
    space between them.
  7. Bake to 165 degrees in center: bake in preheated oven until cooked through, about 45 – 50 minutes, while
    broiling during the last few minutes for better browning.
  8. Serve when hot with rice or potatoes and some vegetable salad
